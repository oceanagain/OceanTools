What does dControl.exe do?

It disables Windows Defender/Security but it's 100% optional.

Tutorial: 
1. Open Windows Security then Click Virus & Threat Protect, Then Manage Settings and Turn Off "Real Time Protection" and "Tamper Protection."
2. Open dControl.exe,  Click disable "Windows Defender" then click "Menu" and add to the "Exclusion List"

False Flags/Virus Detection: 
1. Due to the nature and purpose of dControl.exe disabling windows defender of course it gets flagged. 
2. Dcontrol is made by Sordum a known and reputable website known for creating popular software like DNS Jumper, PowerRun, Edge Blocker and More!
